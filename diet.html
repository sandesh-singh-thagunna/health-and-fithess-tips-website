Best Diet Practices

Fresh Fruits & Vegetables

Eat at least 5 portions of fruits and vegetables daily for essential vitamins and minerals.

Balanced Meals

Include a healthy mix of proteins, carbs, and healthy fats in your diet. Avoid junk foods.

Stay Hydrated

Drink 2-3 liters of clean water daily to stay energized, flush toxins, and boost metabolism.
